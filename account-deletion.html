How to delete your Trade Audit App account and data.

Trade Audit App allows user account deletion within the app. For information about the types of data associated with a Trade Audit account, review the Privacy Policy.

Deleting a Trade Audit account will immediately delete all user added information, with the exception of:

  • Access logs, including IP address
  • User feedback explicity submitted by the user account through the Trade Audit App
  • Crash logs and general system information, and anonymized performance information

Open the left side navigation

This will open the Account Page

On the Account Page

Click the Delete Account button.

This screen will delete all user data.
